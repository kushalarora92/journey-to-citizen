# Delete Your Account - Journey to Citizen

## How to Request Account and Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your Journey to Citizen account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

---

## Steps to Request Account Deletion

1. **Send an email** to: **support@journeytocitizen.com**
2. **Subject line**: Account Deletion Request
3. **Include the following information**:
   - Your registered email address (the one used to create your account)
   - A brief statement confirming you want to delete your account and all associated data

4. **Verification**: We may contact you to verify your identity before processing the deletion
5. **Processing**: Your request will be processed within **30 days**
6. **Confirmation**: You will receive an email once your account has been permanently deleted

---

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be **permanently deleted**:

- Your account information (email address, display name)
- Your profile data (PR date, immigration status, country of origin)
- Your travel history and absence records
- Your physical presence records in Canada
- All analytics and usage data associated with your account

---

## Data Retention

- **Immediate deletion**: Most data is deleted within 30 days of your request
- **No data is retained** after processing your deletion request, except where required by law (e.g., financial records for tax purposes, which are retained for the minimum period required by applicable laws)
- **Backups**: Data in system backups will be deleted within 90 days

---

## Important Notice

⚠️ **Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.**

Before requesting deletion, make sure you have exported or saved any data you wish to keep. Once deleted, your account and all associated information cannot be recovered.

---

## Questions?

If you have questions about account deletion or data privacy, please contact us at **support@journeytocitizen.com** or review our [Privacy Policy](https://journeytocitizen.com/privacy).
