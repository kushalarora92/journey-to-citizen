# Privacy Policy for Journey to Citizen

## Introduction

Journey to Citizen ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you use Journey to Citizen, you may provide:

- **Account Information:** Email address, display name
- **Immigration Details:** PR date, immigration status, country of origin
- **Travel History:** Dates of travel outside Canada, destinations
- **Presence History:** Dates of presence in Canada before PR, purpose of stay

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** App features used, screens viewed, session duration
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Analytics Data:** Anonymous usage patterns to improve the app

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Core Services**
   - Calculate citizenship eligibility
   - Track physical presence in Canada
   - Store and manage your travel history
   - Display personalized dashboard and insights

2. **Improve the App**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new features

3. **Communicate with You**
   - Send important updates about the app
   - Respond to support requests
   - Provide service-related notifications

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored

- **Firebase Firestore:** All personal data is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform servers located in the United States
- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted both in transit (HTTPS) and at rest

### Security Measures

- **Authentication:** Firebase Authentication with email/password
- **Access Control:** Only you can access your data through authenticated sessions
- **Firestore Security Rules:** Database-level security prevents unauthorized access
- **No Third-Party Access:** We do not share your data with third parties

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained as long as your account is active
- **Account Deletion:** You can request account deletion at any time by contacting support@journeytocitizen.com
- **Deleted Data:** Permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** View all data we have about you
- **Correction:** Update or correct your information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export:** Request a copy of your data (coming soon)
- **Opt-Out:** Disable analytics tracking in app settings

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Firebase (Google)

- **Purpose:** Authentication, database, analytics, hosting
- **Data Collected:** Email, user ID, usage data
- **Privacy Policy:** https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### Expo

- **Purpose:** App development platform
- **Data Collected:** Crash reports, performance metrics
- **Privacy Policy:** https://expo.dev/privacy

## Children's Privacy

Journey to Citizen is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app:

- **Data Collected:** Screen views, button clicks, feature usage
- **Purpose:** Improve user experience and fix issues
- **Anonymous:** Analytics data is not linked to your personal information
- **Opt-Out:** Available in app settings (coming soon)

## Cookies and Tracking

- **Web Version:** Uses browser local storage for authentication persistence
- **Mobile Apps:** Uses secure device storage for session management
- **No Advertising Cookies:** We do not use advertising or tracking cookies

## International Users

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers in the United States. By using Journey to Citizen, you consent to this transfer.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):
- We comply with GDPR requirements
- You have additional rights under GDPR (contact us for details)
